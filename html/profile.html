About

Hello! I am Oliver Olah, founder of GuitarLessonsPWA. I developed this web application for the guitar learners to experience a new and enhanced way of learning via PWA technology. It is to enable the learners to interact with the tutoring materials more efficiently, with a greater ease but most importantly to improve the learner's environment for a better engagement. I believe that it is much easier to learn the guitar in the quality environment.

I hope you will enjoy the web application!
